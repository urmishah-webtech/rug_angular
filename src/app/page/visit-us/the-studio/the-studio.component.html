The Studio, Marrakech

Rug shopping, reimagined. Witness how Moroccan rugs are woven and washed, design your own, and top it all off with an espresso (or rosé) from our Milan-inspired bar.

The Beni Experience.

Two years after launching this digital home of Beni Rugs, we broke ground on an 8,000 square foot studio in Tameslouht, Morocco. The studio - which houses our design HQ, rug showroom, and our team of incredible Berber women who weave the brand’s creations - is situated on the Route to Amizmiz, an emerging design enclave twenty minutes south of the bustling city of Marrakech.

A new way to shop

The studio aims to modernize the traditional set-up of shopping handcrafted Moroccan rugs within the city’s markets. Here, our rugs are displayed on sleek modular rack systems, and an immense selection of yarns and color swatches inspire customization.
