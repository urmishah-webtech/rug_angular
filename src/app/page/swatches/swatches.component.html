Our traditional pile is sumptuous, warm, and incredibly soft underfoot. The coziest addition to any room. The pile for these rugs ranges from 1" - 1.5".

Our flatweave pile is the breezier, more casual cousin to full pile. A different weaving technique means these rugs keep a lower profile. Easy to layer and at home in any setting.
